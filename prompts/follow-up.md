# Follow-Up Email Prompt

Use this prompt when drafting follow-up emails for prospects who haven't responded
or who need continued nurturing.

---

## Instructions for Claude

Draft a follow-up email for:

**Contact:** {{first_name}} {{last_name}}, {{title}} at {{company_name}}
**Previous outreach:** {{previous_activity_summary}}
**Days since last contact:** {{days_since_contact}}
**Deal stage:** {{stage}}

### Follow-up sequence strategy:

**Follow-up 1 (3–4 days after initial email):**
- Bump the thread (reply to your original)
- Add new value — a relevant case study, seasonal trend, or gallery link
- Keep even shorter than the first email (2–3 sentences)

**Follow-up 2 (7 days after follow-up 1):**
- New angle — reference something timely (upcoming holiday season, trade show, industry trend)
- Offer something tangible — free mockup, sample tin, catalog
- Still short

**Follow-up 3 (14 days after follow-up 2):**
- "Breakup" email — acknowledge they're busy, leave the door open
- Mention you'll circle back in a few months (seasonal timing)
- Move to nurture stage in pipeline

### Seasonal hooks for Stannum Can follow-ups:

| Month | Seasonal Angle |
|-------|---------------|
| Jan–Feb | Valentine's Day tins, spring product planning |
| Mar–Apr | Mother's Day, Easter, spring gift sets |
| May–Jun | Summer seasonal, Father's Day, wedding favors |
| Jul–Aug | Back-to-school, fall planning, early holiday orders |
| Sep–Oct | Holiday tin season (Christmas, Hanukkah) — PEAK OUTREACH |
| Nov–Dec | Last call for holiday orders, New Year planning |

### Re-engagement (for nurture/cold prospects):

Use when circling back to a prospect after 3+ months:
- Reference original conversation
- Share something new (new tin shapes, new capability, relevant case study)
- Low-pressure: "Thought of you when we launched our new {{shape}} line"

### Tone guidelines:
- Never guilt-trip about not responding
- Never say "just checking in" or "circling back" (overused)
- Always add new value in every follow-up
- Keep it human and conversational
- One question maximum per email
