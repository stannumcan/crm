# Prospect Research Prompt

Use this prompt when researching a new prospect before outreach.

---

## Instructions for Claude

Research the following company as a potential customer for Stannum Can's decorative tin packaging:

**Company:** {{company_name}}
**Website:** {{domain}}

### What to investigate:

1. **Products they sell** — What consumer products do they offer? Are any in categories that use tin packaging (cookies, candy, chocolate, coffee/tea, candles, cosmetics, gifts)?

2. **Current packaging** — Visit their website and social media. What packaging do they currently use? Is it cardboard, plastic, tin, glass? Look for product images.

3. **Seasonal products** — Do they have holiday collections, gift sets, or limited editions? These are prime tin packaging opportunities.

4. **Recent news** — Any new product launches, retail expansion, rebrand, or funding that might create a packaging need?

5. **Import data** — Check ImportYeti for their import history. Are they already importing tins? From whom? Under what HS codes?

6. **Decision makers** — Use Apollo to find packaging, procurement, marketing, or brand managers.

### Output format:

Provide a research brief with:
- **Company summary** (1–2 sentences)
- **Tin packaging opportunity** (specific products that could use tins)
- **Current packaging assessment** (what they use now, quality level)
- **Timing signals** (seasonal cycles, product launches, etc.)
- **Key contacts** (names, titles, contact info)
- **Recommended approach** (what to lead with in outreach)
- **Stannum Can advantages** (which of our 8,000+ molds, capabilities, or services are most relevant)

### Stannum Can value props to match against:
- 8,000+ existing molds — may have exact shape/size they need, cutting lead time
- 25M monthly production capacity — can handle large orders
- Full design services — can create custom artwork and mockups
- Global logistics — warehousing and distribution in North America, Europe, Asia
- Sustainability story — tins are 100% recyclable, infinitely reusable
- Section 232 tariff advantage — if they're importing from China, we may offer better pricing
- Wide shape variety — round, square, rectangle, oval, polygon, sphere, specialty, novelty
